Beginning of a legend

You bid your family farewell. As a firebender that has come of age, it is now finally your turn to join the proud ranks of the Fire Nation Army. As you walk to the the nearby dock to board the ship that will take you to the Training camp. You remember the expectations that you carry on your shoulders. Being a descendant of an esteemed Military Family you know you are expected to do great things. You have witnessed the beauty and warmth of fire and are eager to continue your training and master it. Yet, you have also seen the ferocity and destruction that fire causes.You know that in the army you will learn how to be a powerful weapon that your enemies will fear.
As you get closer to the ship you notice that there is another boat on the far side of the docks. You recognize its colors and flag, it is the travelling circus. The persons on that ship have travelled and explored the world.
Will you get on the ship and follow in your family's powerful legacy. Or will you travel the world?
