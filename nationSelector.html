Beginning of a legend


Long ago, the four nations lived together in harmony.
Then, everything changed when the Fire Nation attacked.
Only the Avatar, master of all four elements, could stop them,
but when the world needed him most, he vanished.

Fifty years have passed and the world has begun to lose hope that the avatar will ever return.
The people of the world now face a new world, a divided world.
Young benders from all over the world have all but forgotten of the avatar
....and must now make their own legend.
