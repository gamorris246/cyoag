Water Tribe

The element of change. The people of the Water Tribes are capable of adapting to many things. They have a sense of community and love that holds them together through anything.

Earth Kingdom

The element of substance. The people of the Earth Kingdom are diverse and strong. They are persistent and enduring.

Air Temple

The element of freedom. The Air Nomads detached themselves from worldly concerns and found peace and freedom.

Fire Nation

The element of power. The people of the Fire Nation have the desire, will, energy and drive to achieve what they want.
