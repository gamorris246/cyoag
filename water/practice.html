Beginning of a legend

The Water Bending masters that were to teach you have all left to help out in the War. You practice as best you can, trying to figure out the basics on your own. It is very difficult without a teacher.

After a rather uneventful session you see a small quick glow in the distance out of the corner of your eye. By the time you focus in that direction it has disappeared.
Was it perhaps your imagination. Maybe its just a reflection from some ice.

Do you ignore it or go over to investigate?
